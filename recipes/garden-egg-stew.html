Garden Egg Stew

Description

Garden egg stew is a flavorful West African dish made with garden eggs (a type of small eggplant), tomatoes, onions, and spices, often cooked in palm oil. It has a rich, slightly smoky taste and is commonly paired with boiled yams, plantains, or rice. An absolute delicacy.

Ingredients

  • 8-10 White/Green Garden Eggs OR
  • 2 Medium Aubergines/Eggplants
  • 1/2 Cup Palm Oil
  • 2 Big Red Bell Peppers
  • 1 Big Tomato (Optional)
  • 2 Scotch Bonnets
  • 1 1/2 Medium Onions
  • 1/2 Cup Smoked Prawns
  • 1/2 cup Dried Eja Shawa Fillets ( You can also use shredded Smoked Fish)
  • 3 Tablespoons Ground Crayfish
  • 1 Maggi Crayfish or any Bouillon Cube of your choice
  • Salt to Taste

Steps

  1. Using the pulse function on your blender, blend your garden eggs roughly and set aside. You can also use a mortar and pestle to pound roughly.
  2. Blend the bell peppers, scotch bonnet, tomato if using any and 1 whole onion roughly and set aside.
  3. Add the palm oil into a medium pot, place on a hob on medium heat, when itâ€™s hot, add 1/2 sliced onions, sautÃ© till fragrantâ€¦
  4. Now add the blended pepper mix, Maggi crayfish and salt to taste, fry for 10-12 minutes.
  5. After 10-12 minutes, add the Shawa or any dried or smoked fish of your choice, also add the crayfish and smoked prawns, fry for 2-3 minutes. Do stir occasionally to prevent burning.
  6. Then add the garden eggs. Cover and cook for 5-6 minutes, till garden eggs soften.
  7. And it's ready.
